Annual Goal #1: By the end of the IEP year in SY24-25, during structured and unstructured opportunities, given a visual daily schedule on a touch screen tablet with speech generating device software or a hard copy visual daily schedule, the student will independently identify his/her upcoming classes during daily routines.

Objective 1: The student will independently identify his/her upcoming classes during daily routines. He/She will respond to the question "What class are you in now?" with 100% accuracy with fading prompts in 4 consecutive trials for each class.
